About PixelConvert

What PixelConvert is

PixelConvert is a modern, high-performance web utility built to make image processing simple, fast, and secure. We provide a suite of tools that allow you to convert, compress, and extract text from images without the need for complex software or risky server-side uploads.

Tools we offer

We provide a comprehensive set of image utilities tailored for modern workflows:

  • Format Conversion: Seamlessly switch between HEIC to JPG, WEBP to JPG, PNG to JPG, and JPG to PNG.
  • Image Compressor: Reduce file sizes significantly for web use or email without sacrificing visual quality.
  • Image to Text (OCR): Powerful Optical Character Recognition to extract text from scanned documents or photos.
  • Images to PDF: Combine multiple image files into a single, professional PDF document.
  • Favicon Generator: Instantly create website icons in all standard sizes from a single logo.

Privacy-first approach

At PixelConvert, your privacy isn't just a setting—it's our core architecture. Unlike most online converters, your files are never uploaded to a server. All processing happens locally in your browser's memory using technologies like WebAssembly and the Canvas API. This ensures that your private photos and sensitive documents stay exactly where they belong: on your device.

Who it’s for

PixelConvert is designed for anyone who values efficiency and security:

  • Students: Quickly convert lecture photos or merge scanned notes into PDFs for submissions.
  • Content Creators: Optimize images for social media or convert modern formats for editing software.
  • Online Sellers: Compress high-res product photos to meet marketplace file size requirements.
  • Office Professionals: Securely extract text from receipts or documents without leaking data to third-party servers.

Limitations

While we strive for perfection, please keep in mind:

  • OCR Accuracy: Text recognition results can vary depending on image quality, lighting, and font complexity.
  • Device Performance: Since processing happens on your device, very large files or batch operations may take more time depending on your computer or mobile phone's hardware.

Updates & feedback

We are constantly refining our algorithms and adding new features based on user needs. If you have suggestions or encounter an issue, please feel free to reach out through our Contact page. Your feedback helps us keep PixelConvert the best free tool on the web.
