About Our Service

'Image Converter Tool' is a web-based utility designed to help anyone edit images easily, quickly, and securely.

Many online conversion sites upload user files to a server for processing. This can lead to slow speeds and significant privacy risks.

This service utilizes modern web technologies (WebAssembly, Canvas API, etc.) to perform all operations directly within your browser. Your valuable photos are never sent anywhere; they are processed exclusively on your own device.

Key Features:

  • Various format conversions including HEIC, WEBP, PNG, and JPG
  • Image size optimization (Compression)
  • Text extraction from images (OCR)
  • Merging multiple images into a single PDF
