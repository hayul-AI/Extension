About PixelConvert

PixelConvert is a state-of-the-art web utility designed for fast, secure, and professional image processing.

Unlike traditional online converters that upload your private photos to a remote server, PixelConvert performs all operations 100% locally within your browser. This means your data never leaves your device, ensuring total privacy and near-instant processing speeds.

Our mission is to provide high-quality creative tools that are accessible to everyone, for free, without the need for registration or software installation.

Key Technologies:

  • WebAssembly (WASM) for high-performance OCR
  • HTML5 Canvas for lossless image conversion
  • Tesseract.js for localized text extraction
