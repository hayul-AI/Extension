Privacy Policy

At PixelConvert, your privacy is our top priority. Our service is designed from the ground up to be "Local-Only."

1. Data Processing

All image conversions, compression, and OCR (text recognition) tasks are performed entirely within your web browser. We do not upload your images to any remote server.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect personal information, email addresses, or any image data. Since everything runs locally, we have no access to the files you process.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your visit to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. OCR and LanguageTool

The "Check Grammar" feature in our OCR tool uses the LanguageTool API. If you use this feature, only the extracted text is sent to their public endpoint for analysis. No images are sent.
